Planned Backend Features

New Bloom is designed to grow into a full AI-powered self-discovery platform. These are the core backend features that will bring the experience to life.

🔐 User Accounts & Profiles

Users will be able to sign up, log in, and create a personal profile. This allows them to save their readings, come back to past reports, and track their growth over time.

🧠 AI Reading Engine

A core system powered by AI that generates personalized readings based on numerology, astrology, and user input. This includes paragraph-style insights, "what this means for your life right now," and full report summaries.

🔢 Numerology Calculation System

Backend logic to calculate all core numbers including life path, expression, soul urge, personality, maturity, and personal year/month/day. Each number connects to detailed meanings stored in the system.

🌌 Astrology + Birth Chart API

Integration with an astrology API to generate accurate birth charts, including moon and rising signs, planetary placements, houses, and aspects.

📊 Saved Reports Database

A database to store user data, generated reports, AI readings, and subscription status so users can revisit their insights anytime.

💳 Subscription & Payments

Secure payment system (Stripe) to manage subscriptions, unlock premium features, and track active plans.

📥 PDF Report Generator

Users will be able to download a beautifully designed version of their full report to keep or share.

🔔 Smart Notifications (Future)

Daily, weekly, and monthly personalized notifications based on personal day numbers, astrology transits, and AI insights.

About Me

New Bloom came from my real love for self-discovery, deep research, and wanting to help people understand themselves in a more meaningful way.

🌸 My Story

I created New Bloom because discovering numerology felt personal for me. It helped me reflect on who I am, how I move through life, and why certain patterns kept showing up. The more I learned, the more I realized that self-discovery can be powerful when it feels personal, beautiful, and easy to connect with.

I am someone who naturally likes to research deeply and look beneath the surface. I have always been drawn to learning more about life, hidden meanings, personal growth, and the deeper story behind who people are. That is a big part of why I wanted to create a page like this.

✨ Why I Made New Bloom

I decided to create this page to help others with self-discovery because I know how powerful it can feel when something finally helps you understand yourself better. Sometimes people are looking for clarity. Sometimes they are looking for healing, direction, confirmation, or just a better understanding of their own energy and path.

I wanted New Bloom to be a space that feels uplifting and soft, not overwhelming. A place where people can explore numerology, astrology, birth charts, and AI personal readings in a way that feels beautiful, inspiring, and full of life.

🔢 Why Numerology Stands Out To Me

Numerology stands out to me because it feels like more than just numbers. To me, it feels like a mirror. It can reflect your strengths, your lessons, your purpose, and parts of yourself you may have always felt but never had the words for. That made me want to build something that helps other people feel seen too.

New Bloom is my way of blending self-discovery with beauty, reflection, and personal insight so people can not only learn more about themselves, but also feel more connected to who they are becoming.

💗 What I Hope People Feel Here

I want people to come to New Bloom and feel like they found a space made with intention. A space where they can pause, reflect, and get insight that feels personal to them. I want this page to help people bloom into a deeper understanding of themselves, their purpose, and the season of life they are in.

This is not just about information for me. It is about connection, self-awareness, and helping people feel a little more guided on their journey.
